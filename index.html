ABOUT

My 2 big passions in life: Web & Travel. I try to explore them in every possible way.

Since my 18th birthday, I have always wanted to discover new beautiful places around the world. I have been able to combine my love for travel with my avidity to understand the functioning of the Web.

During 2 amazing years in Budapest, I have learnt all I know about webmarketing. I took part in more than 80 webmarketing campaigns for various clients of my former Web agency. Since then, I keep learning new things on SEO, SEA & SMO.

Then, I wanted to learn a new language… Programming! I took part in a 9-week fullstack program in Le Wagon Lille. That's how I became a Back-End Developer in a startup in Brussels. There, I could master the backend logic through Ruby on Rails and work hand in hand with the frontend team to deliver new fantastic features to our users.

After this experience where I was challenged to discover the more technical stakes of a Website, I want to go back to Webmarketing. I want to combine my Webmarketing and Programming skills to enable meaningful companies to reach their goal through an efficient and personalized website and webmarketing strategy.
